About Our Project

ElectroMart is built for a smooth and simple electronics shopping experience.

ElectroMart is a Django-based ecommerce project designed to make online electronics shopping easy for customers. The platform allows users to browse products by category, view product details, add items to cart, complete checkout, and continue to online payment in one connected flow.

Our goal with this project is to create a practical shopping website that combines a clean customer experience with useful store management features. Along with the storefront, ElectroMart also includes admin-side tools for managing categories, products, customer contact messages, and order-related activity.

What Customers Can Do

Browse categories, search products, manage carts, place orders, and move through checkout and payment easily.

What The Project Offers

A complete learning-focused ecommerce workflow with product listing, contact support, and admin management features.
